## Ingredients
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/4 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 cup salted butter
- 3 overripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- 1 splash vanilla
## Steps
1. Mix dry ingredients together.
2. Mash bananas and eggs together until smooth.
3. Fold in the wet ingredients.
4. Pour into parchment-lined loaf pan.
5. Bake at 350 F for 50-60 minutes.

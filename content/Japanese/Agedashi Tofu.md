## Ingredients
- 1 block firm tofu
- 1 cup dashi stock
- 2 tablespoons mirin
- 2 tablespoons shoyu
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
## Steps
1. Drain tofu and pat dry with a paper towel and let sit for 30 minutes to dry.
2. Dice
3. Coat in potato starch and fry in hot oil
4. Mix remaining ingredients in a small sauce pan and heat until dissolved.

## Ingredients
- 2 bundles of spinach
- 3/4 cup toasted white sesame seeds
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 1/2 table spoons shoyu
- 1/2 teaspoon sake
- 1/2 teaspoon mirin
## Steps
1. Boil spinach
2. Combine dressing ingredients in mortar and pestle
3. Add to cooled spinach.
